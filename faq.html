Frequently Asked Questions

Food Truck TrackR is an application that brings people closer together through local cuisine. We allow users to find the nearest food trucks avaiable in their areas. This helps office workers find lunch for the day,lends busy tourists a local treat, and caters to every food enthusiast's search for a great meal.

Is Food Truck TrackR Free?

Food Truck TrackR is free for users. Sign up today and save all your favorite food trucks in your favorites list.

Vendors may also sign up for free to use the app. Vendors are charged a 5% commision fee once they set the location of their choice.

How do I find Trucks?

Once you've signed into the app, Food Truck TrackR will display a map with gps locations of all the nearest trucks enrolled in our program.
